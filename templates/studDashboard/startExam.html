Important Notice

Failure to follow these rules may lead to immediate disqualification. Ensure your environment is secure.

Exam Rules & Regulations

  • Full Screen Mode: The test will open in full-screen. Do not exit or switch tabs. Leaving full-screen or switching windows will auto-submit your test.
  • No Refreshing: Do not refresh the page or click the back button during the test.
  • Browser: Start the test only on the Google Chrome browser for the best experience.
  • Submission: Clicking "Submit Test" will finalize your answers. You cannot return once submitted.
  • Auto-Submit: If the timer expires, your saved responses will be automatically submitted.
  • Webcam Monitoring: Snapshots will be taken. Keep your face clearly visible. Poor lighting or missing face may lead to rejection.
  • Behavior: Do not look away repeatedly. Do not use phones. No other people allowed in the frame.
  • Anti-Cheating: Copy-paste, screenshots, and developer tools are blocked and monitored. Violations are logged.
