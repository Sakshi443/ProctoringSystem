About

AI-Powered Remote Proctoring System

Proctor is an open-source, AI-powered remote proctoring platform built to ensure the authenticity and fairness of online examinations. It combines intelligent face detection, head pose estimation, and browser lockdown to prevent cheating and uphold academic integrity.

Our system continuously monitors the candidate’s webcam feed and performs real-time image analysis to detect impersonation, absence, multiple individuals in the frame, and loss of attention. These features help maintain a secure and distraction-free testing environment.

Proctor is designed with low-bandwidth support, making it accessible even in limited internet scenarios. It is fully customizable, easily scalable using AWS, and ideal for institutions and educators who aim to deliver secure online assessments at scale.

Whether you're a candidate appearing for an exam or an educator conducting one, Proctor provides a reliable, user-friendly, and secure platform that ensures the integrity of every test.
